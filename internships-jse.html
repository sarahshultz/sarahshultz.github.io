Jane Street Entertainment

  • Jane Street Entertainment is a full-service television production company based in New York City. Headed by executive producers Donna Macletchie and Linda Lea, JSE continues to produce hit shows such as Cousin's Undercover, Sweet Genius, and Love at First Swipe. With new show ideas being cultivated annually, Jane Steet Entertainment continues to develop and produce captivating lifestyle programming.

As a development and production intern for Jane Street Entertainment, I gained immense knowledge about all parts of production, starting with development. One of my dialy tasks was to research possible show ideas, find credible background information, and even help think of show names! Along with development tasks, I also went on daily runs throughout the city to pick up equipment, drop off wardrobe, and so forth. This was one of my favorite parts of my internship because I learned how to navigate the city while juggling many different pick ups and drops off in a timely manner.

Another exciting part of being a JSE intern was being lucky enough to listen in on casting calls with the producers and even getting to become a culinary production assistant on Rachael Ray's Kids Cook Off. Overall, my time at Jane Street Entertainment has taught me so much in many aspects of development and production ,and has greatly prepared me for my next adventure.
