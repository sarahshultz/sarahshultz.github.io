Who We Are

The Learn Easy team is Ava Gerardo, a junior emerging media major with a computation concentration, Sara Shultz, and Ian Manglaviti, who are both sophomore design and production majors. Together we created a website to help young kids with their education outside of class in a fun way.

The Future

As of now, Learn Easy only has math as its subject and a simple game for easy mode. As a future product, we plan to include four other subjects. They include history, art, grammar/spelling, and science. Introducing different games and difficulties for each game and subject will further advance a child's education.

  LEARN EASY

An easily accessible website intended to connect students, teachers, and parents through online education. Our premeir game, "Space Math" is a fun and free alternative to traditional math comprehension books. With the goal to design a community of eager learners, Learn Easy is for everyone!
