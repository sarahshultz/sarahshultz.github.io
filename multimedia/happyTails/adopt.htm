Things to consider before adopting a dog

1. How old are the members of my family?
2. Who will be the dog's primary caretaker?
3. Can I afford all of the necessities and more?
4. What time of dog is best for me?
5. Will my schedule accomdate my new best friend?
6. Am I a patient person that will not take my frustraion out on my dog?
7. I understand that shelter dogs come with baggage.
8. Where will my dog stay when I am away?
9. I understand accidents happen, pee AND poop.
10. Am I ready for a furry 4-legged best friend?
