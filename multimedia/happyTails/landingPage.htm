Our Mission

Happy Tails’ mission is to connect you to your local animal shelter, providing a convinent way to schedule walks with your local shelter's dogs, all in the comfort of your own home or on the go!

1. Create your Happy Tails Volunteer Account
2. Browse your local shelter dog's profiles
3. Choose a dog that matches your walking pace
4. Schedule a walk with your new best friend
and choose a date and time that fits your schedule.
5. Head over to your shelter and get ready to walk your new furry best firend

So what are you waiting for?
