Taronga Conservation Society
Media Conservation Communications Intern
March 2016 - June 2016

Taronga Zoo

  • Taronga Zoo is located in Sydney, Australia and was the first public zoo to open in New South Wales. I specfically worked within their strong conservation program, where they focus on sustainability, education, and overall positive action to combat the effects aganist illegal poaching, plastics build up, and many more wildlife issues.

As an intern, I worked directly with Conservation Communications Manager, Belinda Fairbrother to create engaging multimedia platforms to educate zoo visitors about different wildlife topics. Our main focus was redesigning the copy for Taronga's For The Wild campaign. My time was spent brainstorming engaging and creative ways to make issues such as sustainable palm oil and illegal wildlife tracking, worth protecting to all demographics.

I also had the privilege to assist with Chris Shepherd's visit to Taronga, the South-East Asian Director of TRAFFIC. Together, Taronga and TRAFFIC have created the Wildlife Witness app. A mobile app that allows users to report illegal wildlife trade by taking a photo, pin the location, and send these details to TRAFFIC. In turn I helped create a visual text video promoting the app that was featured on Taronga's Facebook.
