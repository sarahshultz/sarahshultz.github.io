Taronga Conservation Society

ABOUT

Taronga Coservation Society is located in Sydney, Australia and was the first public zoo to open in New South Wales. Sarah specifically worked within Taronga's Conservation Program, where they focus on sustainability, education, and overall positive action to combat the effects against illegal poaching, plastics build up, and many more environmental issues.

DUTIES

As an intern, Sarah worked with Conservation Communications Belinda Fairbrother to create engaging multimedia platforms to educate zoo visitors about different wildlife sustainability topics. She also had the privilege to work on an initiative between Taronga and the wildlife trade monitoring network, TRAFFIC. The two communities worked together to create the Wildlife Witness app. A mobile app that allows users to report illegal wildlife trade by taking a photo, pin the location, and send these details to TRAFFIC. Sarah helped create a visual text video for the Wildlife Witness mobile app.
