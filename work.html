Assistant to Rachel Goldenberg

ABOUT

Applying for the postion of assistant to Rachel Goldenberg during her last semester of college in Spring 2017, Sarah had the privilege of assisting Racehl for over a year, helping her in any way she could. From learning the ropes of a PA on her first 'offical' shoot when Rachel directed a commercial, to advancing her organizational management skills, Sarah's time with Rachel changed her life. One of the most valuable skills Sarah has gained was learning how to anticpaite someone's needs and executing them before they were even requested, in turn the director's tasks easier.

DUTIES

As an assistant, Sarah has learned countless indispensable skills. Her daily duties include managing Rachel's itinerary and assisting with Rachel's upcoming feature, Valley Girl. The pre-production stages were filled with meetings, location scouts and overall preparation for the five week shoot. During production Sarah helped Rachel with general production duties, i.e. communicating with crew or simply making sure Rachel stayed healthy on the consecutive fourteen hour night shoots. Often you could find Sarah moving around the set with two backpacks (one for her, one for Rachel), making sure Rachel never lost important informatioin during the shoot. Now in post, Sarah assists Rachel through research for Valley Girl related tasks, general assistant duties, coding a new website for Rachel, and even holding paws with Rahcel's cat, Elizabeth!






Taronga Conservation Society

ABOUT

Taronga Coservation Society is located in Sydney, Australia and was the first public zoo to open in New South Wales. Sarah specifically worked within Taronga's Conservation Program, where the focus was on sustainability, education, and overall positive action to combat the effects of illegal poaching, plastics build up, and many more environmental issues.

DUTIES

As an intern, Sarah worked with Conservation Communications Belinda Fairbrother to create engaging multimedia platforms to educate zoo visitors about interesting wildlife sustainability topics. She also had the privilege to work on an initiative between Taronga and the wildlife trade monitoring network, TRAFFIC. The two communities worked together to create the Wildlife Witness app. It is a mobile app that allows users to report illegal wildlife trade by taking a photo, siting the location, and sending these details to TRAFFIC. Sarah also helped create a visual text video for the Wildlife Witness mobile app.






Rachael Ray's Kids Cook Off

ABOUT

Rachael Ray's Kids Cook Off was a show produced for the Food Network by Jane Street Entertainment. It featured eight of America's most talented kid chefs from across the country over an eight week competition. The winner received their own online cooking segment on the Food Network site and scholarship funding towards pursuing a culinary career.

DUTIES

As a culinary assistant, Sarah helped the culinary producer with everyday tasks concerning food preparation, buying, and most importantly, 'greeking'. Along with preparation, the culinary team helped decorate the set for special episodes with fun 'food monsters', refilled the on-set pantries, and cleaned kitchen stations between competitions.






Jane Street Entertainment

ABOUT

Jane Street is known for producing hit shows such as ,Cousin's Undercover', ,Sweet Genius', and ,Love at First Swipe'.
