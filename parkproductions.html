Park Productions.

Located at Ithaca College, Park Productions is a professional video production company that allows students to work with industry professionals to gain real-world experience.
  • Since September 2014, I have worked in various positions at Park Productions. I mainly work as a Production Office Coordinator where I organize logistics of different projects, schedule interviews, and collaborate with other staff to make sure they have everything they need for shoots. I also have experience editing, where I have edited videos for Naturalist Outreach, a video series produced by Park Productions for Cornell University's STEM program.
  • I also had the privilegie to work as a production manager for Geena Davis' Keynote Presentation at Ithaca College in Spring 2014, where I helped film Geena Davi's Interview for Park Productions. Most recently I was a pre-production producer for IC Beyond Body, a media-awareness campaign that encourages everyone to challenge media's representation of self image and focus on inner qualities. Overall I enjoy every part of my time at Park Productions but thoroughly enjoy producing the most and hope to gain more experience in my last semester at Ithaca College.
