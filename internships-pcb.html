Priscilla Costa Bridal

  • Located in the suburbs of Philadelphia, Priscilla Costa is a full service atelier that creates bespoke dresses and made to order collections for all special occasions. This small botique wedding shop brings to life the feeling of genuine compassion torwards making bespoke dresses at affordable prices.

As a social media intern at Priscilla Costa my knowledge of the wedding world grew immensely, along with how to market it on social media. My main goal was to help grow Priscilla Costa's social media presence, especially in the local community. From taking pictures of the boutique, to creating scheduled posts on HootSuite, learning when and how to word each post within differnet social media platforms proved diligent and sometimes quite hard. Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest were the main social media platforms used.

One of my favorite parts with my time at Priscilla Costa was getting to sit in on consultations and fittings, where I was able to hear each client's story, see their bespoke weddding dress, and eventtually get to retell their story through digital media.
